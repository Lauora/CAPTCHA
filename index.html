ᑕᗩᑭTᑕᕼᗩ

CAPTCHA is a full form for "Completely Automated Public Turing Test to Distinguish Robots from People.“ CAPTCHAs typically involve challenges, such as typing distorted characters, selecting specific images, or solving puzzles, to verify the user's identity and prevent automated bots from accessing or interacting with a website. The goal is to ensure a higher level of security by distinguishing between human users and automated scripts.
